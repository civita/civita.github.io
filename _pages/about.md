Hello, I'm Michael Chang, a dynamic and results-driven software professional with a strong foundation in Computer Science and Physics. Holding a Master's in Computer Science from [the University of Texas at Austin](https://www.utexas.edu/) and a Master's in Computer Science from [National Taiwan University](https://www.ntu.edu.tw/english/), alongside a Bachelor's in Physics, I bring a rich blend of theoretical rigor and practical engineering experience to the table.

In my current role as a Software Engineer at [Oracle NetSuite](https://www.netsuite.com/portal/home.shtml), I specialize in runtime performance optimization, developing extensible AI integration toolchains, and scaling high-concurrency backend services. This builds upon my earlier journey, which includes architecting robust automated testing frameworks as an intern at Oracle, and designing containerized Go services as a Full Stack Developer at An-Hui Information Technology. My technical approach is heavily informed by my academic research in hardware simulation and high-performance computing, reflected in my academic achievements, including a stellar GPA of 3.97/4.00 at the University of Texas and 4.25/4.30 at National Taiwan University.

With a versatile toolkit that includes Java, C++, Go, Python, and TypeScript, coupled with deep expertise in distributed systems, runtime environments, and CI/CD pipelines, I am well-equipped to navigate the complexities of modern software architecture. Let's connect to explore how my skills and experiences can contribute to your team's success.
